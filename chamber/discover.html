Discover Nairobi

About Nairobi

Demographics

Population: Nairobi is home to over 4.5 million residents, making it one of the most populous cities in Africa. Its diverse population reflects a blend of cultures and ethnicities, contributing to its vibrant economic and social landscape.

Languages Spoken: Swahili and English are the official languages, widely spoken alongside many local dialects.

Age Distribution: The city's youth population is substantial, with over 60% under the age of 30, indicating a dynamic and growing workforce.

Local Attractions

Nairobi National Park: A unique wildlife experience where you can see lions, rhinos, and giraffes just outside the city center.

Kenyatta International Conference Centre (KICC): The iconic building in Nairobi hosts business events and offers a panoramic view of the city.

The Nairobi National Museum: Explore the rich history of Kenya, from archaeological finds to contemporary art.
