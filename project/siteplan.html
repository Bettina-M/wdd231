NaiBites Siteplan

Overview

Purpose

Naibites is a food odering website for residents living in Nairobi. Its a one stop place where all fast foods from different restaurants can be ordered and delivered within a short time. It's convinient for the busy individuals/families who have less time for cooking.

Scenarios

  • Which restaurants offer food at friendly prices?
  • Is there a one stop place where i can get to know menu for different restaurants?
  • Where can i order food and have it delivered within the shortest time possible?
